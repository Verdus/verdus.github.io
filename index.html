About Me

Hello! My name is Austin Fuller, and I am a design major at Eastern Washington University. I enjoy reading books, watching movies, and drawing pictures in my free time.

Designs

I usually use a semi-minimalistic design style, and I often try to break my designs into few colors. I enjoy pairing serif and sans-serif fonts, and my favorite pairing is Garamond and Futura. I believe that simplicity is key to delivering a message, even though I enjoy complex art.
